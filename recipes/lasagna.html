The BEST Lasagna Recipe

If you love lasagna, you're going to want to make this! It's DELICIOUS and full of flavor!
Try this and let me know what you think! I tend to lean more towards using ground chicken or maybe ground turkey
but you never know what you may come up with on your own. :)

Ingredients

  • 1lb Ground Meat (Chicken, turkey, beef, you pick!9
  • 1 Egg
  • 1 box of lasagna noodles
  • 1 8oz can of crushed tomatoes
  • 1 4oz can tomato puree
  • 1tbsp Sea Salt
  • 1 tsp Italian seasonings
  • Ground pepper, to taste
  • 1 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1 onion
  • 1 12oz container ricotta cheese
  • 1 bag shredded cheese
  1. Pre-heat your oven to 325 degrees.
  2. Warm a skillet on medium heat. Add in 1tbsp of olive oil. Add in the ground meat and season.
  3. Next, chop up your onions and once the meat is browned, toss in the onions as well to cook.
  4. Prepare the tomatoes and tomato puree and add into the mixture in the skillet.
  5. Layer the noodles on a greased pan, followed by the cheeses, and then the meat mixture. Repeat this step.
  6. Bake in the oven for 30 minutes.
  7. Enjoy!
