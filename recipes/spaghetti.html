Homemade Spaghetti Recipe

Prepare your tastebuds for something amazing. Spaghetti is a well known comfort food, and a personal
favorite of mine. I love it almost any time of the year! I know a lot of people that also love spaghetti,
so I don't think you can ever go wrong with this one! It's easy, too!

Ingredients

  • 1 box of spaghetti noodles
  • 1 lb ground meat
  • 1 can petite tomatoes
  • 1 Small onion
  • 1 can of BERTOILLI spaghetti sauce

Instructions

  1. Start by placing water into a sauce pan to boil for the noodles. Also began chopping your onion.
  2. Next, began to brown the ground meat of your choice in prepartation for the sauce.
  3. When the meat has browned, place the chopped onion into the same skillet to cook.
  4. Once that is done, put the tomatos and tomato sauce into your pan to warm.
  5. Boil the noodles as instructed on the box.
