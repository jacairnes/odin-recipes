Enjoyable Baked Ziti Recipe

Baked Ziti isn't something I always think to make, but when I DO make it, I am so glad I did!
It's very much a comfort food, best for when you're not TOO worried about your calorie intake for the day. ;)

Ingredients

  • 3/4 lb Sausage
  • 1 Onion
  • Ziti Noodles

Instructions

  1. First, prepare the meat by cooking it in a skillet.
  2. Next, begin to boil the noodles.
  3. Finally, once both of the above steps are done, put these 2, meat and cheese into a pan and bake at 350 for 40 minutes.
